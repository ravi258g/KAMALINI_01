Our Story

Kamalini by Manusha Reddy is more than a saree brand — it is a heartfelt tribute to India's timeless handloom heritage. Founded with passion and purpose, Kamalini celebrates the artistry of master weavers from across the country, bringing their centuries-old techniques to modern wardrobes with grace and authenticity.

Each saree in our collection — from intricate Pochampally Ikat and Pen Kalamkari to delicate Jamdani, luxurious Chanderi, and vibrant Kutch embroidery — is handcrafted with love, using traditional methods and natural materials whenever possible. We believe in preserving endangered crafts, empowering artisan communities, and offering pieces that carry stories of culture, skill, and soul.

Manusha Reddy's vision is simple yet profound: to make every woman feel connected to her roots while embracing elegance that transcends trends. Whether it's a temple visit, festive celebration, or quiet everyday luxury, Kamalini sarees are designed to make you feel beautiful, rooted, and timeless.

We invite you to explore our collections, feel the craftsmanship, and become part of this journey of tradition reborn.
